LET'S LEARN ABOUT WASTE CONTROL!

Waste control involves classifying waste by its type (solid, liquid, hazardous, or organic) and applying the required management technique:

🛍️

Prevention / Reduction

Minimizing the amount of waste generated at the source.
Using cloth bags instead of plastic bags is a good example.

♻️

Recycling

Processing waste materials into new products.
Recycling paper and plastic as a suggestion.

🫙

Reuse

Repurposing items to extend their lifespan.
We can use old jars as storage.

⚕️

Recovery

Extracting energy or value from materials that cannot be repurposed.

🗑️

Disposal

Landfilling or incineration as a last resort.
